Holy Family Private Nursing LLC

Catholic/Christian Nursing and/or Hospice Care in your home by experienced Catholic nurses.
We address mind, body, heart and soul in our practice and incorporate prayer,
as well as hands on care for all our patients.


  • More Information

Holy Family Private Nursing was started by Christy Burns,
a Catholic mom of eight children, whose nursing experience includes
hospice services, wound & ostomy care and serving the aged
and dying Veterans in the VA.

We want to offer our presence and experience to you & your loved ones,
working in conjunction with your Provider and/or home care nursing service.
We provide general nursing services, hospice care-including crisis interventions,
and wound care for pressure injuries, surgical wounds (including wound vacs),
leg ulcerations, diabetic foot ulcers as well as ostomy care & teaching.

>

For hospice care, we can travel/fly to your location for a one-week commitment (or more).

We work in all the states included under the RN Nursing Licensure Compact (NLC).

NLC is enacted & awaiting implementation: States of Ohio (1/1/23), Pennsylvania, Virgin Islands.

Please note that we are unable to accept Medicare or Health Insurance for the care that we provide.

Exception states (non-comparct states where we cannot work): Hawaii, Oregon, Nevada, Washington DC, California (Christy has a RN license in CA), Alaska, Washington, Michigan, New York, Massachusetts, Rhode Island, Illinois.
